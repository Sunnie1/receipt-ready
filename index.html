Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to sort anything?

No. Just gather your receipts and send them.

How is pricing calculated?

Pricing is $149 per 100 receipts. After your package is received, receipts are counted and your final total is confirmed before work begins.

What if I do not know how many receipts I have?

You may count them before mailing, or send the stack as-is. Get Receipt Ready will count the receipts after arrival and confirm the final total before organizing begins.

Do you prepare taxes?

No. Get Receipt Ready provides receipt organization services only.

Will I get my original receipts back?

Yes. Your original receipts are returned as professionally organized receipt records.

Can I request a binder?

Yes. Professional binder assembly is optional.

What about faded thermal receipts?

Some receipts fade over time, especially thermal paper receipts. Get Receipt Ready will handle receipts carefully, but faded, damaged, or unreadable receipts may not be fully legible.

Can I mail receipts from anywhere?

Yes. Get Receipt Ready is designed as a mail-in service.
